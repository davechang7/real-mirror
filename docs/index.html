Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026  ·  App version 1.0

Real Mirror ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera images. When you use the Skin Scan, Eyebrow Analysis, or Face Workout features, the app captures a photo from your camera. That image is transmitted to Google's Gemini AI service for analysis and is not stored by us on any server.
  • Analysis results. The AI-generated text response is saved locally on your device only so you can review past assessments. This data never leaves your device except as described above.
  • No account or registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifying information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Camera images are sent to the Google Gemini API solely to generate your analysis. We do not retain copies of your images.
  • On-device analysis history is used to display your past results within the app.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API. Image data is processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles API data.
  • Cloudflare Workers. API requests are routed through a Cloudflare serverless proxy to protect our API credentials. Cloudflare may log request metadata (IP address, timestamp) per their standard terms. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Associates. Product recommendations may include Amazon affiliate links. If you click a link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Amazon may set cookies on your device. See Amazon's Privacy Notice.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store your data on our servers. All analysis history is stored locally on your device. To delete it, clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Real Mirror is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
